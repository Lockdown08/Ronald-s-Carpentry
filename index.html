Ronald's Carpentry

Your Home For Exelent Woodwork


Ronald’s Carpentry is a skilled and organized small business, operating in the heart of Johannesburg. They deal with wood working, furniture, repairs and cabinets. Established in 2012 the company has set itself apart from being your regular carpentry shop focusing on delivering in house work and remote work as well. With over 10 years of experience, Ronald Carpentry excels in designing and fabricating woodwork. Our business specializes in kitchens, cupboards, bar counters, wall units, and shelving. We offer both standard sizes and custom-crafted items, proudly installing throughout Johannesburg and delivering within Gauteng.
