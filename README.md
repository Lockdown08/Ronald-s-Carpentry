# 3 Project overview
Ronald’s Carpentry is a skilled and organized small business, operating in the heart of 
Johannesburg. They deal with wood working, furniture, repairs and cabinets. Established in 2012 the company has set itself apart from being your regular carpentry shop focusing on delivering in house work and remote work as well. 
# 4 Website Goals and Ojectives
Increase the company’s reach throughout the different provinces in the country. 
Bring in use of online quotations and bookings, thus making acquisitions of the sevices needed easy. This will also make a csv list which will place customers in need of services in order helping the company know which customer to attend to first . 
Bring in potential new customers looking for carpenter with experience. 
Showcase past projects on the site and reviews from previous customers to create a sense of trustworthiness. 
Have an online portfolio for individuals and big organizations to peruse through. 
# 5 Key features and functionality
Home Page 	Showcasing a welcome note and overview of services rendered, an image slider with some of the finest work will be displayed. 
About us  	I shall be incorporating a small section into the website tell the history, present and future of the company. 
Gallery Page 	Showcasing of all the woodwork done, custom furniture and anything to do of completed projects with a caption tag on them. 
Services page 	A description of the services rendered such as, cabinets, custom furniture and repairs. 
Contact section below the webpage 	This should have the address, contact number as well as the email for customers needing to get in touch with the business. 
Testimonials 	Reviews from previous customers stating their experience with the company. 
Quote request page 	Clients can access this section and request price for a custom job. 
SEO  	Basic search engine for local visibility.
Make carpenter a keyword to rank in the top 10 searches in the results.
Social media linking 	Buttons at the bottom of the page that open a link to Facebook, X and Instagram social pages.
